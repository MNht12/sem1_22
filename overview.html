Project Information
(Overview)

MamaFood is an online community where Vietnamese people can discover new places to eat and drink, including restaurants, eateries, cafes, bars, and bakeries, but also local ingredients and pop-up street food in the area. Members of MamaFood can also share content like images, videos, articles about places, dishes and recipes, or even steam their own cooking show on our platform. In addition, users can comment, share, interact with the posts and support their favourite content creators if they are interested in the content they are watching. Besides that, we also offer cooking tutorials from many famous chefs around the world, who will share their tips and tricks for cooking more efficiently with you. They will teach you everything, even someone with no experience with cooking will go from just boiling an egg to preparing a perfect "Sesame and Coriander Crusted Basa". Currently, there are two ways for people to access the community, which is the website or our app (MamaFood™) is available on Android, IOS and Windows.

One of our primary advantages is that we deliver food quickly and have a variety of cuisines suitable for students. We've contacted a number of eateries and businesses that provide student discounts. In Vietnam, for example, high school students frequently take supplementary classes. Some of them barely received a thirty-minute lunch break before returning to their lessons. As a result, our service is useful; we provide rapid delivery from local eateries and cafés, as well as a discount for customers who have a student card.
